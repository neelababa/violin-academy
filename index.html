Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age for kids?

I typically start students from age 7 onwards. My lessons focus on building an encouraging connection to keep them motivated.

Do you help with buying a violin?

Yes. I assist with procurement, maintenance, and even negotiate with vendors to ensure you get the best value instrument.

How do online lessons work?

We use high-quality audio/video platforms with multiple camera angles so you can see fingerings and bowing technique clearly.

You can choose either,We follow Suzuki/ABRSM for Western and Planned structured curriculum for Indian Classical.
